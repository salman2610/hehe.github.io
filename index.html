A leading diagnostic center in Kerala, required a modern technology platform to automate their scanning services, manage appointments efficiently, and enhance patient engagement. Team Sesame stepped in to design and develop a fully automated solution with dynamic service listings, integrated appointment forms, and a user-friendly admin panel for content management and reporting.

Outcome: With this custom-built application, the client achieved a 70% reduction in manual handling of inquiries and updates, significantly faster appointment bookings, and seamless online report delivery. The optimized performance and mobile responsiveness further improved user experience and operational efficiency.
